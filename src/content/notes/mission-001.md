# Mission briefing

The USS Orchid is investigating a simple question:

> How can one individual gain the functional capacity of an organization without surrendering sovereignty over the self?

Follow the signal in order:

1. [[Kansei Engineering]] turns felt experience into system parameters.
2. [[Computational Semantics]] turns language into inspectable meaning.
3. [[AAC]] turns internal meaning into viable communication.
4. [[Cybernetics]] turns communication into feedback and continued agency.
5. [[Distributed Autonomous Operator]] names the augmented individual.
6. [[Q.U.I.L.L.]] organizes the layers around one operator.

The route is curated. The constellation is not. Leave the path whenever another star starts pulling harder.
