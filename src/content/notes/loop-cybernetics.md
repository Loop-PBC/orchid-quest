# LOOP Cybernetics PBC

> A venture research firm working to build augmentative systems that extend individual agency through computational tools.

LOOP studies and develops operator-centered cybernetic systems using software, language, automation, artificial intelligence, human-machine collaboration, and other computational tools.

Its current focus is The Q.U.I.L.L. Project, an open-source framework for personal augmentation and [[Distributed Autonomous Operator|distributed autonomous operation]].

LOOP's research constellation includes [[AAC]], decision science and operations research, [[Computational Semantics]], human-computer interaction, [[Kansei Engineering]], computer-mediated communication, neuroprosthetics, cognitive liberty, decentralized governance, morphological freedom, and self-determination.
