# Augmentative and Alternative Communication

AAC includes methods and systems that supplement or replace speech. Q.U.I.L.L. extends this framing across the full communication chain:

```text
internal meaning → viable communication
```

The barrier may not be the absence of language. It may be the combined work required to retain an intention, recover context, select an identity and register, preserve privacy, phrase the message, choose a channel, send it, track the response, and resume after interruption.

A Q.U.I.L.L. House can externalize those steps while keeping the operator present and authoritative.
