# A DAO for one

A **Distributed Autonomous Operator** is a single individual whose functional capacity is distributed across tools, agents, machines, identities, collaborators, and time while the individual remains the sovereign center.

A conventional decentralized autonomous organization distributes authority across participants.

A Distributed Autonomous Operator distributes the *functions* of one person.

This distinction allows one operator to maintain multiple communication surfaces, persistent memory, delegated work, scheduling, research, and treasury processes without manually impersonating an entire institution.

[[Q.U.I.L.L.]] is the technical framework. A **House** is a named deployment.
